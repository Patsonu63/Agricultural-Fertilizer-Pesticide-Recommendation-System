Crop Management Plan for Rice - Vegetative Stage

Based on the soil analysis and crop requirements, we recommend the following comprehensive action plan for your rice crop:

1. Soil Treatment

Your soil shows moderate acidity (pH 5.8) and needs calcium supplementation to improve structure. Apply:

  • Dolomitic lime: 500 kg/ha to gradually increase pH and add calcium and magnesium
  • Organic matter: 2.5 tons/ha of well-decomposed compost to improve soil structure and water retention

2. Fertilizer Application

Sumit Pvt Ltd's Premium Fertilizer Schedule:

  • Base application: Sumit DAP (18:46:0) at 100 kg/ha immediately
  • First top dressing: Sumit Urea (46-0-0) at 60 kg/ha at 20 days after transplanting
  • Second top dressing: Sumit Urea (46-0-0) at 60 kg/ha at panicle initiation stage
  • Micronutrient spray: Sumit Zinc Foliar at 2.5 kg/ha at 30 days after transplanting

3. Pest Management

For the current stem borer infestation (medium severity), apply:

  • Sumit Cartap 4G at 18 kg/ha, evenly broadcasting in standing water
  • Keep water level in the field at 2.5 cm for 72 hours after application

4. Best Practices

  • Maintain water level of 2-3 cm during vegetative stage
  • Monitor for pests weekly, especially during morning hours
  • Ensure proper spacing between plants (20x15 cm) for optimum tillering
  • Remove weeds manually 30 days after transplanting

5. Expected Outcomes

  • Improved tillering with 18-22 productive tillers per hill
  • Dark green foliage indicating proper nitrogen uptake
  • Resistance to lodging and improved drought tolerance
  • Potential yield increase of 15-20% over standard practices

Follow-up: Please contact our Sumit Pvt Ltd agronomist for a field visit in 14 days to assess the effectiveness of these interventions and make any necessary adjustments.
